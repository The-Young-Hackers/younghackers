What We Do

Connect

We connect and empower young hackers everywhere. Come to one of our hackathons to meet other programmers and our amazing mentors and organizers!

Interact

We encourage everyone to meet new people at different tech scenes and build awesome projects together.

Inspire

We welcome programmers of all levels - we often have workshops at our hackahons to help students new to programming get inspired and learn to code.

Bring Together

We have a strong community of young programmers. Join us by first signing up for our mailing list here
